DO NOT

Electrical Outlet

To avoid fire or shock hazard, plug the appliance directly into a 120V AC electrical outlet.

Rugs

To avoid fire hazard, NEVER put the cord under rugs or near heat registers.

Heat Sources

DO NOT place humidifier near heat sources such as stooves, radiators, and heaters.

Children

NEVER place humidifier in an area where it is accessible to children

Closed Room

NEVER use humidifier in a closed room. Usage in a closed room may result in excessive humidity.

Polarized Plug

The appliance has a polarized plug. One blade is wider than the other. This plug is intended to fit in a polarized outlet only one way. If the plug does not fit fully in the outlet, reverse the plug. If it still does not fit, contact a qualified electrician to install the proper outlet DO NOT ATTEMPT TO MODIFY THIS PLUG OR DEFEAT THIS SAFETY FEATURE IN ANY WAY

Carpets

NEVER place the humidifier on a rug or carpet, or an a finished floor that may be damaged by exposure to water or moisture.

Moisture and Walls

DO NOT allow the Moisture Outlet to directly face the wall. Moisture could cause damage, particularly to wall paper.

Humidity Level

Do not use humidifier in an area where humidity level is in excess of 50%. Use a hygrometer, avsailable at your local retailer or through the manufacturer, to measure humidity.

Overhanging Surfaces

To prevent damage to surfaces, do not place humidifier under overhanging surfaces.

DO

Electrical Outlet Distance

Locate your humidifier on an inside wall near an electrical outlet. The humidifier should be at least 4 inches (10 cm) away froom the wall for best result

Stable, Waterproof Surfaces

Always place humidifier on a firm, flat, level surface. A waterproof mat or pad is recommended for use under the humidifier.

Cord and Plug Installation Safety Instructions

The lenght of cord used on this appliance was selected to reduce the hazards of becooming tangled in, or tripping over a longer cord. If a longer cord is necessary an approve extension cord may be used. The electrical rating of the extension cord MUST BE greater or equale than the rating of the humidifier.
